BT_RuralChiwog — Primary rural class

House 12, Bemithang Chiwog, Nanong Gewog, Pemagatshel Dzongkhag, Bhutan

  • House numbers sequential within chiwog (gaps of 2 for infill)
  • Authority: Gewog administration / Gup office
  • Physical: house plate + chiwog signboard + finger posts

BT_RuralDistance — Farmsteads along gewog roads

House 35, Chapcha Gewog Rd, Chapcha Gewog, Chukha Dzongkhag, Bhutan

  • 50 m unit from gewog road origin (cf. AS/NZS 4819 rural)
  • For structures >200 m from chiwog cluster
  • Position at farm track meeting gewog road
